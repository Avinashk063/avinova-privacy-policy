Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 15 February 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Avinova – Expense Tracker ("the App") handles user information.

1. Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, or share any personal information from users.

2. Local Data Storage

All expense data entered into the App is stored locally on the user’s device using secure SQLite database storage. The App does not transmit data to any external servers.

3. Third-Party Services

The App does not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, tracking tools, or external SDKs.

4. Data Responsibility

Users are solely responsible for managing and backing up their data. Uninstalling the App may result in permanent loss of locally stored data.

5. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated in future versions of the App. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
