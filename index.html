This Privacy Policy describes how Avinova Tic Tac Toe ("the App") handles user information. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it.

1. Data Collection

Avinova Tic Tac Toe does not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, location, or contacts.

2. Local Game Data

The App may store basic game information such as scores or game progress locally on your device to improve gameplay experience. This data remains only on your device and is not transmitted to any external server.

3. Internet Usage

The App does not require an internet connection to play the game. No user data is sent to Avinova servers.

4. Third-Party Services

This version of the App does not use third-party advertising networks, analytics tools, or external SDKs that collect user data.

5. Children's Privacy

Avinova Tic Tac Toe is safe for players of all ages. The App does not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be reflected on this page with a revised effective date.
